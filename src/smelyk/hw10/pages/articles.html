Adopting a cat from a stranger on Facebook?

There is a large private market for the provision of cats (kittens included). This applies to both free rehoming and outright buying and selling. The internet is “flooded” with advertisements and solicitations for both dogs and cats. This market is in principle unregulated in that the item (i.e. the cat) is in many cases transferred and taken over “as is”.

In the vast majority of cases, we hope and believe that a new owner will be happy to bring a cat into their home, but we recommend that this preferably happens through organizations that have documented experience with this.

Types of Common Household Pets

Household pets are animals people keep at home for companionship, care, or enjoyment. They include furry friends, feathered pets, and even small animals that are easy to handle indoors.

Dog
Loyal companion known for guarding, playing, and being part of the family.
Cat
Independent yet affectionate pet that loves warm spots and quiet naps.
Rabbit
Soft, gentle animal that enjoys hopping around and chewing vegetables.
Parrot
Colorful bird that can mimic sounds and sometimes talk.
